# --- Single words/abbreviations ---
touse = top house (as in fraternity/sorority ranking); alternates like "bouse" mean bottom house
chud = a derogatory, joking term for someone who stays inside all day
mogging = outclassing someone, dominating them, typically looks-wise
cooked = 'he cooked you' means he destroyed you, 'he cooked' means he did well
W or L = good or bad, used like "W staff for helping"
glazing = excessively praising someone or something
deadass = seriously, genuinely, no exaggeration
lowkey = subtly, somewhat, not overtly
atp = at this point
cracked = having sex with someone
edon = Co-ed frat
BK = Berkely College
HOC = head of college

# --- Phrase patterns (use [brackets] for variable parts) ---
the big [name] = requesting/hyping a specific person for something, implies they're a big deal (e.g., "can we get the big Kiriakou for YPU?")
bro is trying to [verb] = mocking someone for doing something premature or cringe (e.g., "Bro is trying to pre order")
sheeple = people who follow the crowd mindlessly (e.g., "all the sheeple went to PR")

# --- Emoji combos (how posters express reactions) ---
😭✌️ = "this is crazy, I wouldn't do that"
💀 = "I'm dead" / that's hilarious
😭😭 = exaggerated sadness or disbelief, not literal crying
🤩🤩 = sarcastic excitement or genuine hype depending on context
😬 = cringe or awkward sympathy
